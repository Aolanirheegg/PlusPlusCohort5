Welcome Cohort 22!

January - April 2024

Welcome to the newly revised Code Chica ++ curriculum!

Code Chica believes teaching HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to young Latin-American girls not only equips them with technical skills, but also promotes a mindset of exploration, creativity, and the confidence to participate in the dynamic world of technology. We are commited to building a more diverse and inclusive future in the tech industry.

  1. Empowerment and Inclusivity:

    Providing coding skills to young girls fosters inclusivity in the tech industry, which has historically been male-dominated. It empowers them to pursue careers in technology, breaking gender stereotypes and promoting diversity.

  2. Building Digital Literacy:

    Learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript enhances digital literacy, an essential skill in today's technology-driven world. It equips girls to navigate and actively participate in the digital landscape, whether for personal or professional purposes.

  3. Career Opportunities:

    Acquiring coding skills opens up a variety of career opportunities in web development, software engineering, and related fields. As the technology sector continues to grow, there is a demand for skilled professionals, and encouraging girls to learn these languages prepares them for future careers.

  4. Creative Expression:

    HTML, CSS, and JavaScript are tools for creative expression. Learning to code allows girls to bring their ideas to life on the web. It encourages creativity, problem-solving, and innovation as they design and build their own digital projects.

  5. Problem-Solving Skills:

    Coding promotes logical thinking and problem-solving skills. Girls who learn these languages develop the ability to break down complex problems into manageable parts and find solutions.

  6. Entrepreneurial Opportunities:

    Coding skills empower young girls to create their own websites, apps, or online platforms. It introduces them to entrepreneurship, enabling them to explore and implement their business ideas in the digital space.

  7. Global Connectivity:

    Understanding web development languages connects girls to the global community. They can collaborate on open-source projects, share their work, and contribute to the broader tech community.

  8. Tech Advocacy and Leadership:

    Knowledge of coding positions girls to advocate for diversity and inclusivity in the tech industry. It opens doors for them to take on leadership roles in technology and influence positive change in the field.

  9. Future-Proof Skills:

    In a rapidly evolving technological landscape, coding skills are increasingly valuable. Learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript provides a solid foundation for adapting to new technologies and programming languages in the future.

  10. Community Building:

    Coding skills create opportunities for girls to engage with coding communities, both locally and globally. This sense of community can provide support, mentorship, and inspiration.
